Meet Mariana

Discover Timeless Elegance

At Mangata & Gallo, we believe that every special moment in life deserves to be celebrated with the finest jewelry. As an Austin-based jewelry store with a global reach, we specialize in creating exquisite pieces that symbolize love, commitment, and cherished memories.

Our Story

Founded by Mariana, a talented jewelry designer with a passion for crafting timeless pieces, Mangata & Gallo has grown from a small storefront in Austin to a renowned online destination for those seeking the perfect jewelry for their special occasions.

The Artisan Behind the Jewelry

Mariana, the founder and lead designer of Mangata & Gallo, has a deep passion for jewelry design. With a background in diamond cutting and metal smithing, she creates pieces that not only capture the brilliance of the stones but also tell a unique story.
